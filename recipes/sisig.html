Sisig

A food mixed with different parts of a Pig depends on the version

A popular Filipino dish

Can be considered as a meal or an appetizer

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly
  • Pig Ears
  • Ginger
  • Chicken Liver
  • Seasonings
  • Spices
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Start by boiling both the pork belly and pig ears until tender
  2. Afterwareds grill it for a few minutes until brown
  3. Chop the meat into small pieces after grilling
  4. Start cooking the sisig by grilling the ginger
  5. Add chicken liver
  6. Add the minced meat
  7. Blend all the ingredients together
  8. Afterwards add the seasonings and spices
  9. Wait until cooked to your preference and you're done!
