Fried Fish

A fish that is fried to perfection

This fish is famous in the Philippines

Know for its simple yet satisfying taste

Ingredients

  • Fish
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Vinegar
  • Soy Sauce

Steps

  1. Put the fish in a plate
  2. Put some salt to add taste to the fish
  3. Mix with some vinegar and soy sauce to add flavor
  4. Prepare a pan and heat it up with the oil
  5. Once the oil is hot enough, fry the fish with it
  6. Wait till the fish is cooked or changes to golden brown
  7. Put out the fire and prepare the fish and you're done!
