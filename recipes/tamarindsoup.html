Tamarind Soup

A simple soup that is soothing to your spirit

Famously known as a famous restaurants soup

Been used to compliment dishes from a famous restaurant

Ingredients

  • Knorr Sinigang powder mix
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green Chili

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Mix in the chopped onions, tomato, a whole green chili, and the Knorr Sinigang Mix
  3. Boil till preference
  4. And you're done!
